Reference

1. Blog

The section where the description of author with his image was designed pretty clean. The description of author in the left and his links to social media looked systematic. Hence, I styled my blog's author section in the similar way, although the difference can be seen with the image placing and social media image links, it looks quite similar.

2. Form

I got the idea of designing the form from bluleadz, contact us page. I liked the white background layout of the form with curvy edges, hence I made transparent black background under my form with curvy edges. Likewise the form is placed at the right side whereas I have put it in the middle so that the form can get main attention.

3. Navigation Bar

The navigation bar of mostlyserious website is quite attractive and uinque specially when we scroll down the animated transition of navigation bar looked really school. Therefore, I implemented similar type of navigation function using javascript and it turned out really well. The difference can be seen in styling the navigation bar whereas the transition effect looks similar.

4. Slider

The idea of slider was inspired from the disney website. Whenever I visited that website I found the slider under the content fascinating. The slides of different images showcasing its contents in the website. Hence, I also kept a slider underneath the main contents of the website. I put the slider in the background covered with dim overlay and main contents of the website such as navigation bar, buttons and footers over the slides.

5. Button

The buttons in the website is referred from mockplus, a designing website. The cury edged border in the button with changing in the background color when hovered looks amazing. Hence, i tried imitating the button. Not only that I put box shadow when hovered and looks like button glowing.
